{%- comment -%} giscus mounts via IntersectionObserver so the giscus client.js (and the iframe to giscus.app, which is the single largest third-party payload on the page) only downloads when the comments section approaches the viewport. On a typical desktop visit that's well after LCP/TBT settle. data-theme is resolved at runtime from , falling back to prefers-color-scheme. A static `data-theme="..."` would bake in one URL at build time; dark-mode users would flash light comments before scripts.html could send setConfig. Subsequent theme toggles are postMessage'd to the iframe by scripts.html. {%- endcomment -%}
