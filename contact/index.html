Hi, I’m Harry.

Since 2018, I’ve been consistently writing and sharing content about iOS development, RPA automation, and CI/CD, along with life stories, product reviews, and travel experiences.

By the end of 2025, I’ve published 120+ articles, totaling hundreds of thousands of words.

In addition to writing, I actively develop and maintain several open-source projects, including:

  • ZMarkupParser – A library for parsing HTML and converting it into NSAttributedString
  • XCFolder – A tool that helps migrate legacy Xcode project structures to a modern folder layout
  • ZMediumToMarkdown – A utility for downloading and backing up Medium articles
  • ZReviewTender – A service that monitors App Store reviews and forwards them to Slack in real time
  • linkyee – A simple and free Linktree-like personal page

All of my work is created during evenings and weekends, and every project is built with ❤️.

If my articles or open-source tools have been helpful to you, feel free to support me with a donation and buy me a beer 🍺

Your support helps me stay motivated to keep writing, building, and contributing to the developer community.

Thank you — it truly means a lot.
