#### Murmur

The original intention of running Medium was not to make money, but to enjoy sharing with everyone—sharing technical challenges I encountered, hoping to help developers with similar issues avoid detours, or diving deeper based on my research so I can also learn new knowledge; plus, there are very few Traditional Chinese writers, so I hope to inspire others and create a cycle of mutual learning.

So I have never been too concerned about whether articles can generate income or not. If they do, great; if not, that's fine too. If there is income, I can use it to experiment, buy more services or experiences, and then write articles to share with everyone, creating a continuous cycle.

Since I started writing on Medium in 2018, I have known about the Medium Partner Program. However, from 2018, 2019, 2020… year after year, the Medium Partner Program policy remained unchanged, limiting eligible writers to only a few regions (I remember only Singapore and Japan in Asia). Writers outside these regions had a very [complicated](https://medium.com/tenzblog/%E5%9C%A8%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3%E5%8A%A0%E5%85%A5medium%E4%BB%98%E8%B2%BB%E7%89%86%E7%9A%84%E6%9A%AB%E8%A1%8C%E6%96%B9%E6%B3%95-6be1d0d999aa){:target="_blank"} process to earn money, requiring VPN access to allowed regions plus accounts or phones from those regions. I previously looked into it but found it too troublesome and unsafe.

Many creators have switched to other platforms like Matters, Fanggezi, or set up their own sites with ads to earn revenue. In recent years, Medium has indeed lost many Chinese-speaking creators.

#### Medium Partner Program

Until recently, in August 2024, I accidentally saw an invitation banner in the Medium backend to join the Partner Program (I thought, What? Taiwan is still not open for this). After clicking in, I was surprised to find that it is now fully open, and creators from almost all regions can join the Partner Program and earn revenue from every word of their articles.

> *But it's a bit funny that before you can join the Medium Partner Program to earn money, you first need to pay to become a Medium Member (at least $4 per month).*

### Some Thoughts on Paywall Paid Articles

- Once an article is placed behind the Paywall and becomes a paid article, users must log in and become Medium Members to read the full content. The previously circulating unlimited reading hacks are no longer effective.

- My high-traffic, SEO-friendly articles are mostly unboxings and travelogues; however, the audience is mostly casual visitors who are neither Medium Members nor regular members. They just browse and leave, so these types of articles are not suitable for the Paywall.

- Technical articles are often intended to share information freely and openly, and authors may not consider their content valuable. Additionally, since the audience is mostly industry insiders with low traffic, there is little motivation to add a paywall.  
  **Or provide free links simultaneously so non-Medium Members can also read.**

- Traffic and earnings can be referenced from senior creator [Tenz Shih (施典志)](https://medium.com/u/11162699a102){:target="_blank"}’s [early shared data](https://medium.com/tenzblog/%E5%8A%A0%E5%85%A5medium-payment-program%E4%B8%80%E6%98%9F%E6%9C%9F%E5%B7%A6%E5%8F%B3%E7%9A%84%E6%94%B6%E5%85%A5%E6%83%85%E5%BD%A2-dad68b457d64){:target="_blank"}.

### Medium Partner Program Joining Process

#### 1. [Complete Stripe Account Registration (Open Account)](https://dashboard.stripe.com/register?locale=zh-Hans){:target="_blank"}

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*FqJF7hsBAdYXxNe4dBUNWw.png)

- <https://dashboard.stripe.com/register?locale=zh-Hans>{:target="_blank"}

- I registered a long time ago, so I'll skip the registration process below.

#### 2. Make sure you are a [Medium Member](https://medium.com/manage-membership){:target="_blank"} paying subscriber

First, make sure you have a [Medium Member or Medium Friend](https://medium.com/manage-membership){:target="_blank"} membership.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*9laiOHQ5rnY7U5DHLhu8Pw.png)

- If you don't have one, you need to subscribe by paying first.

#### 3. Go to the [Medium Partner Program](https://medium.com/me/partner/dashboard){:target="_blank"} page to apply for membership

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*j7NUVaESovvsXx_O4Oq29A.png)

- Country Selection  
  Choose your country. Currently, almost all countries are supported.  
  It is assumed that all countries supported by Stripe for payments are available.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*PaNVqsfWPYvxvoZyDl8-1A.png)

- Make sure all three conditions are met:

1. Already a Medium Member ✅

2. Published at least one article in the past six months ✅

3. Region on the allowed list Taiwan ✅

- Click:

1. Confirm you are at least 18 years old

2. Confirm agreement to the terms of use

Click "Enroll now" to proceed to the next step.

#### 4. Complete Medium x Stripe Binding

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*gl95_dE6dcKADzICOHUC7w.png)

- Enter your email address

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*3CbPc2JWktdSBpTvS7NWbw.png)

- Enter your phone number

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*ScYvGm67HDeBRVTTBY9qMA.png)

- Business type: I chose "Individual or Sole Proprietorship"

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*G8lpZ-fhR6p3B7IPbyK30w.png)

- Verify Your Personal Details: Enter your personal information. I was able to pass this step by entering it in Chinese.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*ZdaIPu0wOKJaj15IYPgUfg.png)

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*8zuNpXtNatp5uL6h9RUoUA.jpeg)

Add withdrawal account:

- Account holder name: Enter the English name of the bank account you want to receive payments to

- SWIFT / BIC Code: Enter the SWIFT code of the bank where you want to receive payments. You can [check here](https://swiftcode.info/bankcountryen-taiwan-2/){:target="_blank"}

- Account: Enter the bank account number where you want to receive payments

> *Here, I used a Cathay foreign currency account, directly checked the foreign currency account info in the app, and filled it in. (You can change it later in settings; it seems the verification happens only when the payout is made.)*

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*cFOr2WXcBPP_d2Dv9WvYOw.png)

- Review and Submit: After confirming the information is correct, click "Agree and Submit"

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*YH253qiheEnJ-zrYdaIIvw.png)

After successful submission, you will be redirected back to Medium to continue setting up your tax information.

#### 5. Complete Tax Filing

The following example is based on an individual account without U.S. residency.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*nv9gVnLeFWi9zNo5evqdyA.jpeg)

- Enter your personal information (in full English)

- **Address: Can be translated using [Chunghwa Post Translation](https://www.post.gov.tw/post/internet/Postal/index.jsp?ID=207#result){:target="_blank"}**

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*mP9Z-0rPVHVLB0cA3UnD2g.png)

- Select Tax Form: For non-US citizens without any US status, choose the first option "W-8BEN — for non-US individuals"

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*122Bzn6wwBQOVTRexCqTtQ.jpeg)

- Continue filling in the relevant personal information (in English)

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*t-u07Bfw6JXT_Uz0Izs6ug.png)

- Identification of Beneficial Owner (Continued)

- In this step, directly check "I can’t claim treaty benefits, so I'm not required to provide a Tax ID Number" and click "Continue".

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*Nb7CN_Dy0mL9nDXgu4kY0Q.png)

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*x21YKFPzYeNveB1m8_jDfg.png)

- Claim of Treaty Benefits (Part II)

- At this step, simply click "Continue" to proceed.

- Click "Confirm" to proceed

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*4zFUsFwqRaez2A_GWw4Trg.jpeg)

- After reviewing and confirming the information is correct, click "Continue" to proceed to the next step.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*eXCCCdQq_JYs_UqO9deMVw.jpeg)

Certification (Part III):

- Check all boxes, select "I am signing for myself.", enter your English name, the current date, and your contact email.

- Finally, click "Submit Form" to submit the entire form.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*7KnLk6zd3PwAhV6AyRhY2g.png)

### Put Articles Behind Paywall to Start Earning Revenue

Note that joining the Medium Partner Program alone does not generate earnings; your articles must be placed behind the Paywall to receive revenue shares.

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*qudLUHY7JqphRC_FAodk8g.png)

- Article Editing -> "Manage paywall setting"

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*_ySM_z4wMTd4xYCaJIVtmw.png)

![](/assets/cefdf4d41746/1*n4gRDzM7X0_ISV3gMYSe6w.png)

- Check "Paywall your story" -> "Save"

- Articles participating in the revenue share will have a ⭐️.

This way, the article will generate revenue sharing.
