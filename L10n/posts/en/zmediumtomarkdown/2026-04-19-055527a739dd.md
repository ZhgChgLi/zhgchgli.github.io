#### **Sapporo Station &lt;-&gt; Jozankei Round-Trip Bus Ticket**

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*WwGk0L6ccnJi3FweO4qr_g.jpeg)

![https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/](/assets/055527a739dd/1*i7zUMAOkPm7RwhcYbs3xVA.jpeg)

https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/

There are many JR trains from New Chitose Airport to Sapporo Station, and since the flight arrival time is uncertain, I didn’t buy tickets in advance. To get to Jozankei Onsen, you can only take a bus. According to the [latest transportation information as of December 2025](https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/){:target="_blank"}, there are three route options:

1. **[Reservation Required] [About 60 minutes] Sapporo Station → Take Kappa Liner Bus → Jozankei Onsen.**  
   **Click here to view the timetable**

2. [No reservation required] [About 70 minutes] Sapporo Station → Makomanai Station → Bus → Jozankei Onsen.

3. [No reservation needed] [Approx. 120 minutes] New Chitose Airport → [Airport Bus (Hokuto Kotsu)](https://www.hokto.co.jp/makomanai-chitose/){:target="_blank"} → Makomanai Station → Jozankei Onsen.

**Route (1) — [かっぱライナー号 / Kappa Liner](https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html){:target="_blank"} is the most direct and convenient**. After checking the schedule, the 4:00 PM departure (limited to autumn and winter) leaves plenty of time after landing, passing immigration, and taking the JR to Sapporo Station. So I booked online in advance (foreigners can also book!).

**Outbound Trip Sapporo Station -> Jozankei:**

![<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}](/assets/055527a739dd/1*cAKpufRPO7_kJuG-f3l74w.png)

<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}

- Note that **the trips marked in red only operate in autumn and winter**. The buses departing from Sapporo Station at 16:00 and 17:00 only go as far as the Jozankei Bus Terminal.

**Return Trip Jozankei -> Sapporo Station:**

![<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}](/assets/055527a739dd/1*qVXeuuYsNv__vp_ye6jnvw.png)

<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}

- For the return trip, I planned to check out at 11:00 and stroll around the hot spring street before heading back, so I booked the 13:15 departure (boarding at Shiraito Falls at 13:31) bus.

**Reservation Method:**

1. Go to the [official reservation website](https://jotetsu.quicktrip.jp/buy){:target="_blank"} and register as a member

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*CzG4MgskigUp8NXhe3xExw.png)

- Be sure to remember your account and password, as the e-ticket is activated online directly for the driver to verify.

2. Choose to purchase the [かっぱライナー号 / Kappa Liner](https://jotetsu.quicktrip.jp/buy/reservation?salesTicketId=1943){:target="_blank"} round-trip or one-way ticket

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*HjF2Kh18Rm6CxchhV8rP7g.png)

3. Number of Guests

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*BuuwoHzpfc4R2XeDb-0Luw.png)

4. Select the departure date and flight (flight times are shown as departure from Sapporo)

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*6JVbtozEiw37fj0EqFDADw.png)

5. Choose the boarding and alighting locations for the outbound trip

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*apTSepE2RXgoJmbK_IoFjA.png)

Boarding and alighting stops can be referred to in the chart below:

![<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}](/assets/055527a739dd/1*NyUAFnQb4KqMSoXiRiT6iw.png)

<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}

Our hotel is closer to **(2) [Shiraito Falls/白糸の滝/Shiraitonotaki](https://jozankei.jp/tw/spot/1449/){:target="_blank"}**, so we booked to get off at this stop.

> *Later, I found out you can continue riding the loop and get off at **(7) Jozankei Onsen Higashi 2 Chome / 定山渓温泉東2丁目** stop, which is right in front of the hotel.*

5. Select the return date and departure time (departure times are based on the departure station), as well as the boarding and alighting locations
