#### **Sapporo Station &lt;-&gt; Jozankei Round-Trip Bus Ticket**

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*WwGk0L6ccnJi3FweO4qr_g.jpeg)

![https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/](/assets/055527a739dd/1*i7zUMAOkPm7RwhcYbs3xVA.jpeg)

https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/

The JR from New Chitose Airport to Sapporo Station has many departures, and since the flight arrival time is uncertain, I didn't buy tickets in advance. To get to Jozankei Onsen, you can only take a bus. According to the [latest transportation information as of 2025/12](https://jozankei.jp/tw/news/newaccess/){:target="_blank"}, there are three routes to choose from:

1. **[Reservation Required] [About 60 minutes] Sapporo Station → Take the Kappa Liner Bus → Jozankei Onsen.**  
   **Click here to view the timetable**

2. [No reservation needed] [About 70 minutes] Sapporo Station → Makomanai Station → Bus → Jozankei Onsen.

3. [No reservation needed] [About 120 minutes] New Chitose Airport → [Airport Bus (Hokuto Kotsu)](https://www.hokto.co.jp/makomanai-chitose/){:target="_blank"} → Makomanai Station → Jozankei Onsen.

**Route (1) — [かっぱライナー号 / Kappa Liner](https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html){:target="_blank"} is the most direct and convenient**. After checking the schedule, the bus departs at 4:00 PM (limited to autumn and winter), which is plenty of time after landing, passing immigration, and taking JR to Sapporo Station. I booked it online in advance (foreigners can book too!).

**Outbound Trip Sapporo Station -> Jozankei:**

<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}

1. Register as a member on the [official reservation website](https://jotetsu.quicktrip.jp/buy){:target="_blank"}

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*HjF2Kh18Rm6CxchhV8rP7g.png)

3. Select Number of People

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*BuuwoHzpfc4R2XeDb-0Luw.png)

4. Select the departure date and flight (flight times are shown as departing from Sapporo)

![](/assets/055527a739dd/1*6JVbtozEiw37fj0EqFDADw.png)

Boarding and alighting stations can be referred to in the chart below:

<https://www.jotetsu.co.jp/bus/kappa_liner/index.html>{:target="_blank"}

5. Select return date and departure time (departure time is based on the departure station), boarding and alighting locations
