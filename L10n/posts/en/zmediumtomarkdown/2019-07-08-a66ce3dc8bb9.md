As a clumsy person with OCD, using a delicate product like the Apple Watch is very frustrating; being clumsy makes it easy to accidentally bump it, and OCD means scratches are very upsetting. So, I immediately applied a full-coverage screen protector to prevent accidents.

But actually, **just using a full-coverage screen protector is not enough. The watch has a curved surface, and the edges of the protector are fragile, easily chipping if the watch's bezel accidentally scrapes against something**:

![Full-coverage screen protector edge damage without a case](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*Pqap-5lrHUEWBonbKHXrAA.jpeg)

Full-coverage screen protector edge cracking without a protective case

I have already replaced the full-coverage screen protector three times; although the watch screen itself remains undamaged, it still hurts my heart. Perfect fit + no impact on touch + thin + high transparency + no lifting edges = very expensive ($990 each). The money spent on screen protectors is almost enough for me to upgrade to the stainless steel version. Therefore, an Apple Watch case is very important to me, as it can strengthen the protection of the watch’s edges and reduce damage from impacts.

This article unboxes two Apple Watch protective cases and compares their experience, functionality, appearance, and suitable scenarios. Let's get started!

#### Installation:

We need to first disassemble the Catalyst case, then place the Apple Watch body inside before reassembling it.

1. First, unscrew the screws on the back of the watch

![](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*W-gLZXVO1yJNgXJlNJZmGA.jpeg)

2. After removing the screws, hold the watchband with both hands and use your thumbs to push the watch case outward.

![](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*zuKuxfU47WUxlGtSTdujvw.jpeg)

3. Disassemble all parts

![](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*7BW6I_A_T-1uyz-Enan6jg.jpeg)

![Exploded view (from the [official website](https://www.catalystlifestyle.com/){:target="_blank"})](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*QdX7vbv2I3hgKFofi2CV8Q.jpeg)

Exploded view (from the [official website](https://www.catalystlifestyle.com/){:target="_blank"})

4. Remove the Apple Watch body from the current sport band

![Flip to the back, press the rectangular latch at the bottom with your nail, then push left or right to open!](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*MwDh_iQQNvwLRa-4uZTS4A.jpeg)

Flip it over, press the rectangular latch at the bottom with your fingernail, then push it left or right!

![](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*I_QwQJ6ywR5R6TN8FRSByA.jpeg)

![](/assets/a66ce3dc8bb9/1*DZiQG08CWoAhg7norvU8lw.jpeg)

Make sure there are no wrinkles to avoid affecting the waterproofing.

#### Thoughts:

This watch band truly provides 360° full protection and enhances its waterproof capability to suit harsher environments.

The strap uses the same skin-friendly material, feeling no different from the original Apple Sport Band. However, the adjustment holes are more closely spaced, allowing for a more precise fit (with the original band, I was stuck between a hole that was too loose and one that was too tight). The locking clasp on the keeper also gives me, a perfectionist, extra peace of mind!

The overall look is rugged and wild, perfectly matching outdoor activities like hiking, climbing, and diving. This is exactly the scenario where this strap provides the greatest protection!

### Summary:

It took over four months from receiving the samples to completing this review. During this time, I moved (sorry for the messy photos), participated in a triathlon (10KM running + 40KM cycling), and went diving in Malaysia. These two protective cases have accompanied me through mountains and seas, running, jumping, and more. The full-coverage screen protectors remain flawless!

Remember how many screen protectors I replaced? The answer is 3 protectors in 3 months. On average, each one got chipped within a month for some unknown reason, and each costs $990 Orz.

> I can only say, I wish I had found this sooner. If I had known about protective cases, I wouldn’t have wasted extra money!

Both Catalyst and Muvit solved my problem of screen protector edges constantly chipping. If you don’t use a screen protector, you should definitely get a case to protect the screen edges, or else a cracked screen will hurt even more.

For choice recommendations: if you often engage in intense activities (rock climbing, diving) or manual labor, Catalyst is the safer option; if you are a regular office worker, occasionally run or exercise, and like to change watch bands based on your mood, Muvit is sufficient!
